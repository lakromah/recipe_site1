Recipe

Chocolate cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate : 200 g

Sugar : 150 g

Butter : 200 g

Eggs : 4

Flour :80 g

Instructions

1

Mix dry ingredients

2

Bit the eggs

3

Melt butter together with the dark chocolate for a smooth consistency

4

Mix all ingredients in a big bowl and bake at 180 degrees
